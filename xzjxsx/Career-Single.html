Riding Instructor

Job Overview:

We are seeking a skilled Riding Instructor to join our team. As a Riding Instructor, you will be responsible for providing riding lessons and guiding riders in developing their skills. If you have experience in horseback riding instruction and a passion for teaching, we encourage you to apply for this position.

Job Responsibilities:

As a Riding Instructor, your responsibilities will include:

  • Providing riding instruction to individuals and groups.
  • Assessing riders' abilities and tailoring lessons accordingly.
  • Ensuring the safety of riders and horses during lessons.
  • Teaching proper riding techniques and horse care practices.
  • Organizing and supervising riding activities and events.

Qualifications & Skills:

To succeed in this role, you'll need:

  • Certification in horseback riding instruction or equivalent experience.
  • Proven experience in teaching horseback riding lessons.
  • Strong knowledge of horse behavior, riding equipment, and safety protocols.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Patience, empathy, and enthusiasm for teaching.
